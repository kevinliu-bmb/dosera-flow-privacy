Dosera Flow — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Overview

Dosera Flow is a personal medication tracking app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data Storage

All data you enter into Dosera Flow — including medication names, dose amounts, dose times, and body weight — is stored locally on your device only. Your data never leaves your iPhone.

No Data Collection

Dosera Flow does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs. We have no access to your data.

App Groups

The app uses Apple's App Groups feature to share data between the main app and its home screen widgets. This data sharing happens entirely on your device and does not involve any network communication.

No Tracking

Dosera Flow does not track you in any way. The app does not use advertising identifiers, analytics frameworks, or any form of user tracking.

Data Deletion

You can delete any logged dose from within the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently from your device.

Children's Privacy

Dosera Flow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
